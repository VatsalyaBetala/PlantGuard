Getting Started

1
Upload Your Plant Image

Take a clear photo of the affected plant area or upload an existing image. Ensure good lighting and focus on the diseased parts.

2
AI Analysis

Our advanced AI model will analyze your image within seconds, identifying potential diseases and assessing severity levels.

3
Get Recommendations

Receive detailed treatment recommendations, prevention tips, and actionable steps to address the identified plant health issues.

Best Practices for Accurate Results

High-Quality Images

Use good lighting and focus on the affected areas for better accuracy.

Close-Up Shots

Capture close-up images of symptoms for detailed analysis.

Multiple Angles

Take photos from different angles to show the full extent of the problem.

Early Detection

Analyze plants regularly for early disease detection and prevention.
