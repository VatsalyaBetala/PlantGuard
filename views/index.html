Why PlantGuard

Built for serious plant pathology work

Three things researchers asked for: accurate predictions, transparent explanations, and a workflow that fits into how you already operate.

Transfer-learned accuracy

ResNet-50 plant + per-plant disease heads, fine-tuned on PlantVillage. YOLOv8 crops the leaf first so the classifier never sees background noise.

Grad-CAM explainability

Every prediction ships with a heatmap overlay showing exactly which leaf regions drove the diagnosis — no black-box verdicts.

Fast, offline-capable

~50 ms per image on GPU and runs fully offline on CPU or Raspberry Pi. Deploy to the field, not the cloud.

Pluggable backends

CNN and ViT adapters share the same interface via model_registry — swap architectures with one environment variable.

Agronomy-aware output

Diagnoses are grounded in a curated plant → disease taxonomy and paired with expert-style treatment explanations powered by GPT.

Private by default

Your images stay on your machine. No telemetry, no outbound data, no account required — just a local FastAPI service.

Workflow

From leaf photo to diagnosis in four steps

Designed to mirror how a pathologist actually looks at a leaf — isolate, identify, diagnose, explain.

1

Detect the leaf

YOLOv8 locates and crops the leaf from the uploaded image.

2

Identify the plant

A ResNet-50 plant classifier picks one of six species.

3

Classify the disease

A per-plant disease head returns a labelled prediction with confidence.

4

Explain the verdict

Grad-CAM overlay + LLM-generated treatment guidance accompany the result.
